About Nexus Red Team

Security work runs better when the process is clear.

Nexus Red Team is a security operations platform for assessments, reporting, and awareness training. It gives teams one place to organize tools, findings, and follow-up.

Assessments Reporting Awareness
Problem

Too much context switching

Notes, spreadsheets, and browser tabs slow the work down and make it harder to keep details in order.

Solution

One place for the workflow

Tool reference, finding intake, awareness exercises, and log review stay connected from page to page.

Audience

Built for active teams

Consultants, internal security teams, and independent testers can all use the same structure.
